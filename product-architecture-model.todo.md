## Tachyon CLM, Neutrino CLM, Neutrino Tenancy, Iubki CLM
Tachyon CLM and Neutrion CLM are one and the same; They both operate as one Subscription per Financial Institute semantics.
Development of Tachyon CLM will continue with that name. The SaaS specification will factor-in Neutrino roadmap based requirements as well as Ibuki roadmap based requirements.

Tachyon CLM should therefore have:
- Customer Data Studio
- Decision Application Studio
- Service Relationship Studio

- Service Request Framework (Discovery, Initiation, Tracking, History)
- Customer Interaction Framework (Notify :: Directed Communication, Announce :: Segment Targeted Communication)
- Content Management for Service Relationship Product (Bundle Product)

Neutrino Servicing Realm of an Issuer will have:
- Application Studio (APIs, Events, Data Servicing layers)
- Digital Experience Studio (Mobile, Web)
- Voice Experience Studio
- Servicing Agents Studio
