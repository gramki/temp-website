## Aggregated Data
Much of the Quark, Neutrino, and Ibuki Product lines are to be designed to work with various system of record applications of the customers. The applications are likely to be deeply entrenched in the bank ecosystem and hence cannot be replaced. However, all such applications of a customer may not be talking to each other seamlessly. Customers may or may not have data of these applications collected in an enteprise data lake meaningfully for consumption of other data-driven applications.   

## Originated Data
While the Product Lines are designed to use the data from existing applications of the enterprise, by virtue of creating thier value creation layer, the applications in the product lines themselves "produce" new data. This could be in functional domains that are substituted from the legacy applications or in the functional domains that are newly introduced by these product lines. Such data that is newly produced by these applications is referred to as "Originated Data".


## Enterprise Golden Data Model
For any of the data-driven applications to work, it is important to source and prepare the data for consumption. The list of data consuming applications will be ever expanding. One data model may not fit all. So data is transformed and stored differently to suit the needs of different applications. Quark, Neutrino, and Ibuki represent collection of many such data applications that require to transform and store data for meeting their own use cases. 

There need to be a layer that is agnostic of such usecases that represents the base facts and dimensions, the master data for various entities across the various functional domains of the enterprise.  

* Serving Stores: Operations Serving Store, Customer Service Serving Store, Relationship Banking Serving Store 
* Originated Data to Enterprise Golden Data Model
