# DR-040: Governance Rituals, Enforcement, and Operating Model Foundation

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-05-26

## Context

Governance had been described as transition validation and evidence capture, but the model did not clearly distinguish:

- governance practices and cadences;
- policies and evidence requirements;
- policy enforcement work;
- findings, violations, exceptions, and register entries;
- positive recognition surfaced by rituals;
- evolution of governance practice itself.

Treating every governance action as a generic "Governance Case" made governance look like an exception process and overemphasized policing.

## Decision

1. Model governance definitions in the **Operating Model**:
   - Ritual Definitions
   - Governance Policies
   - Evidence Requirements
   - Cadences
   - Participant Roles
   - Decision Authority
   - Reports / Dashboards
   - Register Definitions

2. Model Governance Track execution through two primary orchestration items:
   - **Governance Ritual**
   - **Governance Enforcement**

3. Classify findings, violations, approvals, evidence bundles, and audit records as **Governance Work Artifacts**.

4. Classify risk, debt, compliance, deferred obligation, and kudos/recognition entries as **Register Artifacts**.

5. Treat **Kudos Register** / Recognition Entries as first-class positive outputs of rituals, with Workforce Repository as the semantic home for recognition.

6. Route evolution of governance practice through **Evolve Cases**:
   - policy updates;
   - ritual updates;
   - dashboard/report updates;
   - playbook updates;
   - changes to cadence, participants, evidence requirements, or decision authority.

## Rationale

Governance should reinforce good practice, not only punish violations. Rituals create shared operating rhythm, surface evidence, recognize strong behavior, and produce follow-up work. Enforcement asserts policies and controls. Findings and register entries persist the results. Evolve keeps governance itself from becoming stale.

## Consequences

- Add Operating Model governance documentation.
- Add Governance Ritual and Governance Enforcement work entities.
- Update ACE governance framing.
- Update Orchestrator and orchestration-item docs.
- Add governance artifacts/registers and clarify that recognition lives in WFR.
- Update Phase 1 open questions to include governance ritual/enforcement MVP decisions.
