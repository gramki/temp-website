# Data Domain

**Model:** Definition Model
**Dimension:** Data
**Owner:** Enterprise Architects, Business Analysts

## Definition

A high-level category of business information — a logical grouping of related Data Entities.

## Purpose

Provides the top-level organization for the product's information architecture. Data Domains align with Modules (Dimension 8).
