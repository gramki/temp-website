# Experiment

**Model:** Work Model
**Track:** Discovery
**Owner:** Product Manager, UX Researcher

## Definition

A formal hypothesis test with explicit pass/fail criteria. An Experiment is designed to produce a definitive signal about a specific assumption behind an Idea.

## Purpose

Provides structured, measurable validation within the Discovery Track. Unlike Research Tasks (which gather evidence broadly), Experiments are designed with a specific hypothesis, method, and threshold for success or failure. They are referenced by Product Decision Records (Dimension 1) as evidence.
