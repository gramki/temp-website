### Revised Assessment

The Axon data fundamentally changed the assessment:

1. **Scale is significant**: 680+ FIs is not a handful of reference customers — it's infrastructure-grade penetration
2. **Revenue mix is product-led**: ~78% SaaS in the US channel is genuine product economics, not services with product aspirations
3. **Distribution model is novel**: Embedding within an incumbent's ecosystem (FIS) is neither the consultative solutions model nor the direct SaaS model — it may represent a distinct archetype
4. **Strategic depth of FIS relationship**: Distribution entrustment (FIS sells Zeta's product as its own) is a signal even beyond strategic investment on the Commitment Ladder

### The Split Identity Finding

The Axon data revealed that Zeta operates fundamentally different businesses in different markets:

- **US (Axon/FIS)**: ~78% SaaS, product-led, distributed through incumbent
- **India (HDFC)**: Revenue mix unknown at this point, but suspected to be services-heavy given the 350-person studio

This "split identity" became the central finding of the subsequent consolidated assessment.
