- **Revenue**: $43.3M ARR (PayZapp + Pixel combined)
- **Revenue mix**: 75% Studio / 17% SaaS / 7% Non-SaaS — operationally a transformation delivery engagement
- **Key finding**: Simultaneous deepening (more products, JV discussions) and contraction (25% studio reduction, insourcing evaluation)
- **Sigma (CLOU)**: Relaunching August 2025 after RBI pause
- **Cross-sell pipeline**: AFA, Diners cards, Sentinel AI, Payments Factory JV

### 3. Pluxee Relationship Analysis

- **Revenue**: $9.6M ARR, "Protect" posture (not growth)
- **Investment history**: Sodexo was Zeta's first external investor (~$65M Series C, 2019) — a customer-investor, not financial
- **Status**: Legacy maintenance engagement from pre-banking era

### 4. Consolidated Assessment — Central Findings

- **Split identity**: Zeta runs at least three distinct business motions — Axon/FIS channel (product-led), direct US enterprise (Optum, others), and India services-led (HDFC anchor)
- **Company-wide SaaS mix**: Estimated ~48% — at the inflection point between services-led and platform-led economics
- **India GTM is product-led** (RFP-driven competitive displacement), not problem-led (compound problem discovery), despite excellent problem articulation in strategy docs
- **Commitment Ladder**: Two customer-investors (Sodexo, Optum), but no banking customer equity investment yet

### 5. Fact Corrections Applied
