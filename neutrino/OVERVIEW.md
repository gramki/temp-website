# Neutrino — Agentic Customer Servicing for Banks

## The Shift Ahead

Customer servicing is entering a new era. AI agents are moving from experimental to essential—and banks that adopt early will set new benchmarks for cost efficiency, service quality, and customer experience.

This shift will unfold in two phases:

| Phase | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Agent-Augmented** | AI agents work alongside your service representatives—surfacing context, drafting responses, and handling routine steps so your team can focus on judgement and empathy. |
| **Agent-Native** | AI agents handle end-to-end servicing autonomously. Customers interact directly with agents—or bring their own agents to transact on their behalf. Agents become a channel, like mobile or voice. |

Most banks will operate in both modes simultaneously for years — serving customers who prefer human touch alongside those who prefer speed and self-service.

---

## What Neutrino Delivers

Neutrino is a purpose-built agentic servicing platform for banks. It provides three capabilities:

1. **Co-Pilot for Service Reps**  
   Augment your contact center and branch staff with real-time guidance, auto-drafted responses, and one-click actions—reducing handle time and training burden.

2. **Autonomous Servicing Agents**  
   Deploy AI agents that resolve common requests end-to-end—account inquiries, card controls, service requests, complaints—without human intervention.

3. **Agentic Channel (A2A / MCP)**  
   Expose secure, policy-governed interfaces so customers' personal AI agents can transact directly with your bank—positioning you for the next wave of digital engagement.

## Built for Coexistence

Banks don't flip a switch from human to agentic. Neutrino is designed for **gradual adoption**:

- Start with co-pilots to boost rep productivity on voice and chat.  
- Add autonomous agents for high-volume, low-risk digital requests.  
- Open the agentic channel (A2A / MCP) when customers demand it.

All channels—voice, email, mobile, web, chat, and agentic—run on a **shared platform**, ensuring:

- **Consistent policies** — the same business rules apply everywhere  
- **Unified audit trails** — every interaction logged, regardless of channel  
- **Seamless handoffs** — escalate from bot to rep, or from digital to voice, without losing context  
- **Single customer view** — conversation history and preferences travel with the customer


## What are the Customer Servicing activities in a bank?

### 1. Inform & Notify
- Keep customers informed (balances, due dates, maturity, rate changes)
- Proactive alerts (unusual activity, low balance, payment reminders, expiry)
- Regulatory/policy communications (T&C updates, fee changes, disclosures)
- Campaign & offer communications

### 2. Answer & Advise
- Answer queries (product, transaction, policy, eligibility)
- Anticipate queries and proactively present answers (contextual FAQs, nudges)
- Provide financial guidance (spend insights, savings tips, credit health)
- Explain transactions (declines, fees, holds, pending items)

### 3. Facilitate Transactions & Self-Service
- Facilitate transactions for the user (transfers, bill pay, recharges, investments, repayments)
- Enable account operations (block/unblock card, change limits, set alerts)
- Manage standing instructions, auto-pay, and mandates
- Support digital onboarding for add-on products (cards, deposits, loans)

### 4. Service Requests & Fulfillment
- Accept and fulfill service requests (statements, certificates, cheque books, PIN reset)
- Process address/contact/nominee/KYC updates
- Handle card replacement, reissue, upgrade, and delivery tracking
- Manage beneficiary and payee setup

### 5. Accept & Resolve Complaints
- Accept and resolve customer complaints (service failures, transaction disputes)
- Track and communicate case status (SLA, next steps, resolution)
- Escalation management and supervisory handoffs
- Proactive service recovery (apology, credit, goodwill)

### 6. Dispute & Claims Handling (Not part of Neutrino; part of Quark: Dispute Operations; When Quark is not used, this is seen as any other user compliant)
- Accept and process transaction disputes (chargebacks, unauthorized transactions)
- Facilitate fraud claims and replacement workflows
- Coordinate evidence collection and updates to customer

### 7. Collections & Dues Support (Not part of Neutrino; part of Quark: Collection Operations)
- Remind and assist with repayments (EMI, credit card dues, overdrafts)
- Offer payment plans, restructuring options, and settlements
- Manage petty loans and micro-credit servicing

### 8. Relationship Lifecycle Management (Not part of Neutrino; part of Quark: Product Growth Operations)
- Dormancy reactivation outreach
- Retention and win-back engagement
- Cross-sell and up-sell based on lifecycle stage and needs

### 9. Feedback & Voice of Customer
- Collect feedback (NPS, CSAT, product feedback)
- Close the loop on feedback with actions or acknowledgements

All of these activities are traditionally scoped to each product a customer is availing from the bank. Neutrino provides a set of agents skilled for handling above activies for each *product family* of the bank.

Example Neutrino Agent Domains:
- Neutrino for Credit Cards
- Neutrino for Savings Accounts
- Neutrino for Checking Accounts
- Neutrino for Personal Loans
