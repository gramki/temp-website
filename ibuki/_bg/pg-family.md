# Objective

# 2. Problem Statement

## 2.1 Current Challenges in Family Payments

### Payment Fragmentation
- Multiple apps/methods for different family payments
- No unified view of family expenses
- Disconnected payment histories across family members

### Control & Monitoring
- Lack of oversight on family member spending
- Difficulty in tracking shared expenses
- No hierarchical approval systems for family purchases

### Family-specific Needs
- No dedicated solutions for recurring family payments
- Complex joint family payment scenarios unsupported
- Limited features for elderly family members

## 2.2 Market Gap

- Existing payment apps focus on individual users
- Banking apps lack family-specific features
- No comprehensive solution for Indian family payment needs

## 2.3 Market Opportunity

- 300M+ households in India
- 150M+ digitally active families
- Growing digital payment adoption
- Increasing multi-generational digital usage

# 4. Analysis & Insights

## 4.1 Payment Patterns

### Frequency Patterns
- Daily transactions (groceries, transport)
- Weekly recurring (allowances, shopping)
- Monthly obligations (rent, utilities)
- Quarterly/Annual commitments (insurance, education)

### Amount Patterns
- Small ticket (₹100-1,000): Daily needs
- Medium ticket (₹1,000-10,000): Regular bills
- Large ticket (>₹10,000): Major expenses

### Control Requirements
- Autonomous: Regular, small amounts
- Monitored: Medium, recurring expenses
- Approval-needed: Large, irregular expenses
- Quick-access: Emergency situations

## 4.2 Persona-Specific Needs Matrix

| Persona | Primary Needs | Control Type | Payment Preferences | Key Challenges |
|---------|---------------|--------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Urban Professional | Automation & Oversight | Dual Control | Digital-first | Time Management |
| Single Parent | Complete Control | Solo Decision | Digital + Monitoring | Emergency Handling |
| Business Family | Mixed Transactions | Family Committee | Cash + Digital | Business-Personal Mix |
| Young Couple | Equal Partnership | Joint Decisions | Full Digital | Expense Splitting |
| Joint Family | Hierarchical Control | Committee Based | Mixed Methods | Multiple Approvals |
| Retired Parents | Assisted Access | Children Support | Assisted Digital | Digital Adoption |

## 4.3 Common Requirements Across Personas

### Payment Management
- Multi-user access
- Flexible approval workflows
- Emergency override mechanisms
- Spending limits and controls

### Monitoring & Tracking
- Family expense dashboard
- Member-wise tracking
- Category-wise analysis
- Budget management

### Security & Access
- Role-based permissions
- Multi-level authentication
- Secure beneficiary management
- Emergency access protocols

### Integration Needs
- UPI payments
- Bill payments
- Investment products
- Insurance services
