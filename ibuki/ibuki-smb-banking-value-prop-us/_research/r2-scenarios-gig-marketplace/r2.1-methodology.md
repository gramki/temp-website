# Research: Platform-Dependent Earners Scenario Research Methodology (Placeholder)

**Research ID:** R2.1 (Platform-Dependent)  
**Date:** 2025-01-15  
**Purpose:** Planned approach for Phase 2 scenario research

## Status

This research will be conducted in Phase 2. This document outlines the planned methodology.

## Planned Methodology

Similar to Professionals scenario research:
1. Secondary research (fintech analysis, platform data, industry forums)
2. Scenario synthesis into journey types
3. Primary research validation across all three subcategories

## Subcategory-Specific Research

**Gig Workers:**
- Fintech analysis (Lili, Moves, Dave, Chime)
- Platform data (Uber, DoorDash, TaskRabbit)
- Gig worker forums and communities

**Marketplace Sellers:**
- Payment processor analysis (Stripe, PayPal, Square)
- Marketplace data (Amazon, Etsy, eBay, Shopify)
- Seller forums and communities

**Content Creators:**
- Creator economy fintech analysis
- Platform data (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitch)
- Creator forums and communities
- Creator economy reports (IAB, industry studies)

## Hypothesized Journey Types (Common Across All)

1. Income Tracking & Smoothing
2. Platform Earnings Management
3. Expense Tracking & Deductions
4. Quarterly Tax Management
5. Income Verification & Credit Building
6. Multi-Platform Coordination
7. Personal-Business Blur
8. Emergency Cash Flow
9. Platform-to-Business Transition

## Subcategory-Specific Journey Types

**Gig Workers:**
- Vehicle/Equipment Costs

**Marketplace Sellers:**
- Inventory Management & COGS
- Multi-Marketplace Coordination

**Content Creators:**
- Equipment & Software Costs
