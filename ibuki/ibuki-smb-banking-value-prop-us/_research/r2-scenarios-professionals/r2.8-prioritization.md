# Research: Professional Scenario Prioritization

**Research ID:** R2.8  
**Date:** 2025-01-15  
**Purpose:** Prioritize scenarios by frequency × impact for MVP and roadmap planning

## Research Question

Which professional financial scenarios should be prioritized for MVP and subsequent phases based on frequency, impact, and coverage gaps?

## Prioritization Framework

Scenarios are prioritized using:
- **Frequency:** How often the scenario occurs (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Annually, One-time, Rare)
- **Impact:** Financial consequence, time cost, stress level (Very High, High, Medium, Low)
- **Coverage Gap:** How poorly banks address the scenario (None, Partial, Full)
- **Priority Score:** Frequency × Impact × Gap Severity

## Prioritization Rationale

**Why These Scenarios for MVP:**

1. **High Frequency + High Impact:** Scenarios that occur frequently and have significant financial consequence
2. **Coverage Gaps:** Scenarios that banks don't address well
3. **Core Needs:** Scenarios that are fundamental to professional financial management
4. **Differentiation:** Scenarios that differentiate relationship-focused banking from product-focused banking

**Why Defer Others:**

1. **Low Frequency:** One-time or rare scenarios can wait
2. **Lower Impact:** Medium impact scenarios can be added later
3. **Better Coverage:** Scenarios that banks address reasonably well are lower priority
4. **Vertical-Specific:** Some vertical-specific scenarios can be added in vertical-specific phases
