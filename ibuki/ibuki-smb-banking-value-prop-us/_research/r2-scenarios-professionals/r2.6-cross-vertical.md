# Research: Cross-Vertical Professional Scenarios

**Research ID:** R2.6  
**Date:** 2025-01-15  
**Purpose:** Scenario inventory - Common scenarios across all professional verticals

## Research Question

What financial scenarios are common across all professional verticals (Healthcare, Legal, Accounting, Consulting, Creative)?

## Key Findings

There are 20-25 scenarios that are common across all professional verticals, representing the core needs that relationship-focused banking should address. These scenarios form the foundation for a unified professional banking offering.

## Journey Type 1: Daily Cash Flow Operations

### 1.1 Real-Time Cash Position Visibility

**User Story:** As a professional, I need to see my current cash position across all business accounts so I can make informed financial decisions.

**Key Needs:**
- Real-time view of all business accounts
- Integration with personal accounts (for owner-operators)
- Cash flow projection (expected income, upcoming expenses)
- Alerts for low balances

**Frequency:** Daily  
**Impact:** High  
**Coverage:** Partial (banks provide balances but not integrated view)

---

### 1.2 Receivables Aging and Collections

**User Story:** As a professional, I need to track which clients haven't paid and manage collections so I can maintain cash flow.

**Key Needs:**
- Track receivables by client
- Identify aging receivables (30, 60, 90+ days)
- Collections workflow
- Impact on cash flow

**Frequency:** Weekly to monthly  
**Impact:** High  
**Coverage:** None (banks don't provide receivables management)

---

## Journey Type 2: Owner Compensation & Draws

### 2.1 When Can I Take an Owner Draw?

**User Story:** As a professional, I need to know how much I can safely pay myself this month given my cash position, receivables, and upcoming expenses.

**Key Needs:**
- Current cash position
- Outstanding receivables and expected payment dates
- Upcoming expenses
- Safe draw calculation
- Historical draw patterns

**Frequency:** Monthly  
**Impact:** Very High  
**Coverage:** None (banks don't provide draw calculation)

---

### 2.2 Planning Owner Draws for Tax Season

**User Story:** As a professional with seasonal income, I need to plan owner draws to smooth income across the year.

**Key Needs:**
- Understand seasonal income pattern
- Calculate draws by month
- Set aside funds for slow months
- Plan for year-round personal expenses

**Frequency:** Monthly (with seasonal planning)  
**Impact:** High  
**Coverage:** None

---

- Owner compensation planning is critical
- Business-personal integration is needed
- Tax planning is important
- Growth decisions need financial support
