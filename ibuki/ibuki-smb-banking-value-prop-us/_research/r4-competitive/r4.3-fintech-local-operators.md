# Research: Fintech Competitive Landscape - Local Operators

**Research ID:** R4.3  
**Date:** 2025-01-15  
**Purpose:** Competitive analysis - Fintechs targeting local operators

## Key Findings

POS/fintech ecosystems (Toast, Square, Clover) dominate merchant services but lack comprehensive banking. Banks have opportunity to integrate banking with merchant services.

## Competitive Analysis

### Toast, Square, Clover

**Target:** Restaurants, retail, local businesses

**Products:**
- POS systems
- Payment processing
- Basic banking (some)
- Business management tools

**Strengths:**
- Industry-specific features
- Integrated POS and payments
- Good for local operators

**Gaps:**
- Limited banking (checking, basic)
- No lending (partner only)
- No business-personal integration
- No scenario-solving

---

## Opportunity

POS fintechs own merchant services but don't provide full banking. Banks can integrate banking with merchant services for comprehensive relationship.
