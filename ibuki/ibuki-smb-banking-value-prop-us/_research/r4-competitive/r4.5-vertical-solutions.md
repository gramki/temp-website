# Research: Vertical-Specific Solutions

**Research ID:** R4.5  
**Date:** 2025-01-15  
**Purpose:** Competitive analysis - Vertical-specific practice management + finance tools

## Key Findings

Vertical-specific tools (practice management software) handle operational workflows but don't provide banking. Opportunity for integration.

## Examples

### Healthcare: Athenahealth, Epic

**Products:**
- Practice management
- EHR (Electronic Health Records)
- Billing
- Some financial reporting

**Gaps:** No banking, no lending, no financial decision support

---

### Legal: Clio, MyCase

**Products:**
- Practice management
- Time tracking
- Billing
- Trust account management (IOLTA)

**Gaps:** No banking (integration only), no lending, no financial decision support

---

## Opportunity

Practice management software handles operational workflows but professionals still need banking and financial decision support. Relationship-focused banking can integrate while providing banking and scenario-solving.
