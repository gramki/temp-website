# Research: Accounting Software Landscape

**Research ID:** R4.4  
**Date:** 2025-01-15  
**Purpose:** Competitive analysis - Accounting software (QuickBooks, etc.)

## Key Findings

Accounting software (QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Wave, Xero) handles bookkeeping and accounting but doesn't provide banking or financial decision support. Opportunity for integration.

## Competitive Analysis

### QuickBooks

**Products:**
- Accounting
- Invoicing
- Expense tracking
- Payroll
- Tax preparation
- Banking integration (read-only)

**Strengths:**
- Comprehensive accounting
- Industry standard
- Good integrations

**Gaps:**
- No banking (integration only)
- No lending
- No financial decision support
- Complex for small practices

---

### FreshBooks, Wave, Xero

**Similar to QuickBooks but:**
- Simpler (targeted at smaller businesses)
- Lower cost
- Less comprehensive

**Same gaps:** No banking, no lending, no decision support

---

## Opportunity

Accounting software handles bookkeeping but professionals still need banking and financial decision support. Relationship-focused banking can integrate with accounting software while providing banking and scenario-solving.
