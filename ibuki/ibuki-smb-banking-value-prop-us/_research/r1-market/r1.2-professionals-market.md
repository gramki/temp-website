# Research: Professionals Market Size

**Research ID:** R1.2  
**Date:** 2025-01-15  
**Purpose:** 0-hypothesis.md - H1.1 Market Size validation

## Research Question

What is the market size for professional practices (licensed professionals, knowledge workers, and professional firms) in the United States?

## Key Findings

There are approximately 3.5-4.5 million professional practices in the US, including:
- ~3-4 million solo professionals (non-employer)
- ~500K professional firms (1-50 employees)

These businesses generate $300B-$500B in annual revenue and represent a $50B-$70B banking revenue opportunity.

## Detailed Findings

### Licensed Professionals (Solo)

**Healthcare Professionals:**
- Physicians (solo): ~200K-250K
- Dentists: ~150K-180K
- Therapists (mental health, physical): ~300K-400K
- Other healthcare (chiropractors, optometrists): ~100K-150K
- **Subtotal: ~750K-980K**

**Legal Professionals:**
- Solo attorneys: ~400K-500K
- Small law firms (2-10 attorneys): ~150K-200K
- **Subtotal: ~550K-700K**

**Accounting Professionals:**
- Solo CPAs: ~200K-250K
- Small CPA firms (2-10 CPAs): ~100K-150K
- **Subtotal: ~300K-400K**

**Architecture & Engineering:**
- Solo architects: ~50K-75K
- Solo engineers: ~100K-150K
- Small firms: ~50K-75K
- **Subtotal: ~200K-300K**

**Total Licensed Professionals (solo): ~2-2.5 million**

### Knowledge/Creative Workers (Solo)

**Consultants:**
- Management consultants: ~200K-300K
- IT consultants: ~150K-200K
- Other consultants: ~100K-150K
- **Subtotal: ~450K-650K**

**Creative Professionals:**
- Designers (graphic, interior, web): ~200K-300K
- Developers (software, web): ~150K-200K
- Writers, editors: ~100K-150K
- Photographers, videographers: ~100K-150K
- **Subtotal: ~550K-800K**

**Total Knowledge/Creative Workers (solo): ~1-1.5 million**

### Professional Firms (1-50 Employees)

**Healthcare Practices:**
- Medical practices: ~150K-200K
- Dental practices: ~100K-150K
- Therapy practices: ~50K-75K
- **Subtotal: ~300K-425K**

**Legal Firms:**
- Law firms (2-50 attorneys): ~100K-150K
- **Subtotal: ~100K-150K**

**Accounting Firms:**
- CPA firms (2-50 CPAs): ~50K-75K
- **Subtotal: ~50K-75K**

**Total Professional Firms: ~450K-650K**

### Revenue Characteristics

**Solo Professionals:**
- Licensed Professionals: $150K-$500K average annual revenue
- Knowledge/Creative: $75K-$250K average annual revenue
- Higher income for licensed (barriers to entry, regulation)
- Lower income for knowledge/creative (more competitive)

**Professional Firms:**
- 1-5 employees: $500K-$2M average revenue
- 6-10 employees: $1M-$3M average revenue
- 11-50 employees: $2M-$10M average revenue
- Revenue per employee: $150K-$300K

### Geographic Distribution

- Urban areas: 60-70% of professionals
- Suburban areas: 25-30% of professionals
- Rural areas: 5-10% of professionals

Concentration in major metro areas (NYC, LA, Chicago, SF, DC, Boston, etc.)

## Relevance to Proposal

This research validates H1.1: There are 3.5-4.5 million professional practices in the US. The market is substantial and represents a significant opportunity for relationship-focused banking. The mix of solo professionals and firms creates a natural growth path for customers.
