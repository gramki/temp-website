
## Family Financial Needs

### Operational
* Payments: Bills, Subscriptions, EMIs, SIPs, Education, (Various Categories of Billers)
* Purchases: PoS, eCOM, ATM Withdrawls
* Events & Projects:
* Entitlements: Salary Income, Rental Receivables, Hand loans, Business Receivables  
* Obligations: All Registered Bills, Loans taken
* Cashflow and Solvency tracker
* Rewards, Incentives
* Warranties, Disputes

### Medium Term
_These needs vary across the life stages of the family members_
* Goals, Plans, Projects
* Investments
* Insurances: Medical, Vechicles, Property
* Treasury: Savings, Loans, Investments, Obligations, Entitlements, and Balance Sheet 

### Long Term (Legacy)
* Immovable Assets
* Property Documents
* Nomination Reviews
* Wills
