## Research Question

What are costs of missed deductions and suboptimal tax strategies for US families?

## Key Findings

US families miss $1,000-$5,000 annually in tax savings through missed deductions and suboptimal strategies. Common misses: retirement contributions, education credits, itemized deductions.
