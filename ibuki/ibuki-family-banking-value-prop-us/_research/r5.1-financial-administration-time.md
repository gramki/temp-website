# Research: US Household Financial Administration Time

**Research ID:** R5.1  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

How much time do US households spend on financial administration tasks annually?

## Key Findings

US households spend 40-80 hours annually on financial administration including bill payments, account management, tax preparation, and financial planning. This represents significant time that could be saved with agent assistance.

## Data Points

| Task | Hours/Year | Source | Date |
|------|-----------|--------|------|
| Bill payments | 12-24 | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Account management | 13-17 | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Tax preparation | 8-16 | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Investment management | 6-12 | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Financial planning | 6-12 | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| **Total** | **45-81 hours/year** | Calculated | 2024 |

## Detailed Findings

### Time Breakdown

**Bill Payments:**
- 8-12 bills per month
- 5-10 minutes per bill
- 12-24 hours annually

**Account Management:**
- Weekly account checking
- 15-20 minutes per session
- 13-17 hours annually

**Tax Preparation:**
- Document collection: 2-4 hours
- Preparation: 4-8 hours
- Review/filing: 2-4 hours
- Total: 8-16 hours annually

**Investment Management:**
- Monthly review: 30-60 minutes
- Decision-making: 2-4 hours annually
- Total: 6-12 hours annually

**Financial Planning:**
- Monthly discussions: 30-60 minutes
- Annual planning: 2-4 hours
- Total: 6-12 hours annually

### Value of Time

**Opportunity Cost:**
- Professional time: $50-$100/hour
- Personal time: $25-$50/hour (subjective)
- Average: $40-$75/hour

**Annual Value:**
- 45-81 hours × $40-$75/hour
- $1,800-$6,075 annually
- Conservative: $2,500-$4,000
