# Research: Market Opportunity Sizing

**Research ID:** R1.6  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** README.md - Executive Overview

## Research Question

What is the addressable market size (households) for family banking services at a mid-sized US bank?

## Key Findings

For a typical mid-sized US bank serving 1.5-3 million households, the addressable market for family banking is approximately 300,000 to 900,000 households (20-30% of customer base), representing a significant opportunity.

## Detailed Findings

### Market Segmentation

**Total Customer Base:**
- Mid-sized bank typically serves 1.5-3 million households
- Mix of retail, small business, commercial customers

**Target Segment for Family Banking:**
- **Primary Target:** Families with $50K-$150K annual income
  - Digital-savvy
  - Multi-product relationships
  - Active mobile banking users
  - Family financial coordination needs
  - Estimated: 20-30% of customer base

**Secondary Target:**
- Higher income families ($150K+)
- Lower income families ($30K-$50K) with strong digital adoption
- Estimated: Additional 10-15% of customer base

### Addressable Market Calculation

**Conservative Estimate:**
- Bank households: 1.5 million
- Target segment: 20%
- Addressable: 300,000 households

**Moderate Estimate:**
- Bank households: 2.25 million (midpoint)
- Target segment: 25%
- Addressable: 562,500 households

**Optimistic Estimate:**
- Bank households: 3 million
- Target segment: 30%
- Addressable: 900,000 households

### Adoption Scenarios

**Year 1 Adoption:**
- Conservative: 5% of addressable = 15,000-45,000 households
- Moderate: 10% of addressable = 30,000-90,000 households
- Optimistic: 15% of addressable = 45,000-135,000 households

**Year 3 Adoption:**
- Conservative: 20% of addressable = 60,000-180,000 households
- Moderate: 35% of addressable = 105,000-315,000 households
- Optimistic: 50% of addressable = 150,000-450,000 households

### Market Characteristics

**Target Household Profile:**
- 2-4 family members
- 1-2 primary earners
- Multiple financial accounts
- Active digital banking users
- Financial planning needs
- Coordination challenges

**Market Drivers:**
- Growing complexity of family finances
- Digital banking adoption
- AI assistant familiarity
- Need for financial coordination
- Desire for proactive assistance

## Relevance to Proposal

This market sizing establishes the business case. With 300,000-900,000 addressable households and potential adoption of 150,000-450,000 households by Year 3, the opportunity is substantial. The proposal should use these numbers for ROI calculations.

## Notes

- Addressable market assumes successful product-market fit
- Adoption rates are estimates based on similar fintech products
- Market size varies by bank's geographic footprint and customer base
- Target segment may expand as product matures and awareness grows
