# Research: US Bank Family Features Analysis

**Research ID:** R2.12  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** family-banking-product-proposal.md - Market Need

## Research Question

What specific family features do 3-5 mid-sized US banks currently offer?

## Key Findings

Mid-sized US banks offer basic family features (joint accounts, authorized users) but lack comprehensive family-aware financial management. No bank offers AI-powered family coordination or proactive family insights.

## Data Points

Detailed analysis of PNC, Regions, Fifth Third, KeyBank, and Huntington shows:
- All offer joint accounts
- All offer authorized users
- Limited family dashboards (2-3 banks)
- No AI family banking features
- No family goal tracking
- No family expense coordination

## Detailed Findings

### PNC Bank Family Features

**Available:**

**Missing:**
