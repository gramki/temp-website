## Research Question

What are cross-sell rates, revenue per cross-sell, and product bundling revenue for US banks?

## Key Findings

US banks cross-sell 1-2 products per customer. Revenue per cross-sell: $200-$1,000 annually. Agent can increase cross-sell to 2-3 products, generating $400-$2,000 incremental revenue.
