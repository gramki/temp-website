# Research: US Bill Payment Time

**Research ID:** R5.2  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

What is the time spent on bill payments including frequency, time per payment, and automation rates?

## Key Findings

US households pay 8-12 bills monthly, spending 5-10 minutes per bill (12-24 hours annually). 60-70% use auto-pay, but coordination and tracking remain manual.

## Detailed Findings

### Payment Methods

**Auto-Pay (60-70%):**
- Setup time: 2-5 minutes per bill
- Ongoing: Minimal time
- Coordination: Still manual

**Manual Payment (30-40%):**
- Time per payment: 8-15 minutes
- Includes: Login, navigation, payment, confirmation
- Higher time cost

### Time Savings Potential

**With Agent:**
- Auto-pay setup: Agent handles
- Coordination: Agent manages
- Tracking: Agent provides
- Time saved: 8-15 hours annually
