# Research: US Churn Rates

**Research ID:** R6.6  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What are annual churn rates, reasons for churn, and retention strategies for US banks?

## Key Findings

US banks experience 8-12% annual churn. Main reasons: better rates elsewhere, poor service, life events. Agent can reduce churn to 4-6% through better service and relationship lock-in.

## Detailed Findings

### Churn Reasons

**Better Rates:**
- 40-50% of churn
- Competitor offers
- Rate shopping

**Poor Service:**
- 20-30% of churn
- Slow response
- Inconvenience

**Life Events:**
- 15-25% of churn
- Moving
- Job change
- Relationship change

### Agent Impact

**Service Improvement:**
- Proactive assistance
- Faster response
- Better experience
- Reduces service churn

**Relationship Lock-In:**
- Accumulated context
- Family coordination
- Switching cost
- Reduces rate churn

**Total Impact:**
- 50% churn reduction
- 8-12% → 4-6%
- Saves 4-6% of customers
