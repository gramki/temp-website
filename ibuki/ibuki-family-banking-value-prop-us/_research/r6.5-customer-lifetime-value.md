# Research: US Customer Lifetime Value

**Research ID:** R6.5  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What is customer lifetime value (LTV), customer retention, and relationship tenure for US banks?

## Key Findings

US bank customer lifetime value is $15,000-$50,000 over 5-10 year relationship. Annual churn is 8-12%. Agent can improve retention and increase LTV.

## Detailed Findings

### Lifetime Value Calculation

**Components:**
- Annual revenue: $500-$1,500
- Relationship tenure: 5-10 years
- Product growth over time
- LTV: $15,000-$50,000

### Churn Impact

**Current Churn:**
- 8-12% annually
- Reasons: Better rates, poor service, life events
- Cost: Lost revenue + replacement CAC

**Agent Impact:**
- Reduces churn to 4-6%
- Saves 4-6% of customers annually
- Value: $600-$900 per saved customer
