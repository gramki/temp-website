# Research: US Tax Preparation Time

**Research ID:** R5.3  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

What is the time spent on tax preparation including complexity and outsourcing rates?

## Key Findings

US families spend 8-16 hours annually on tax preparation. 30-40% use tax preparers ($200-$500 cost), 60-70% use software. Document organization is the biggest time sink.

## Detailed Findings

### Time Breakdown

**Document Collection:**
- Gathering W-2s, 1099s, receipts
- 2-4 hours annually
- Biggest pain point

**Preparation:**
- Using software or forms
- 4-8 hours annually
- Varies by complexity

**Review/Filing:**
- Review and submission
- 2-4 hours annually

### Agent Time Savings

**Document Organization:**
- Agent tracks documents year-round
- Reminds about missing documents
- Organizes automatically
- Saves: 2-3 hours

**Preparation Assistance:**
- Agent provides guidance
- Identifies deductions
- Saves: 1-2 hours

**Total Savings:**
- 3-5 hours annually
- Plus reduced stress
