# Research: US Mid-Sized Bank Customer Economics

**Research ID:** R6.1  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What are average customer value, revenue per customer, and product penetration for mid-sized US banks?

## Key Findings

Mid-sized US banks generate $500-$1,500 annual revenue per customer through deposits, loans, fees, and investments. Average customer has 2.5-3.5 products. Customer lifetime value is $15,000-$50,000.

## Detailed Findings

### Revenue Sources

**Deposits:**
- Net interest margin: 2.5-3.5%
- Average deposits: $20,000-$40,000 per customer
- Annual revenue: $500-$1,400

**Loans:**
- Interest spread: 2-4%
- Average loan balance: $50,000-$100,000
- Annual revenue: $1,000-$4,000

**Fees:**
- Account fees, transaction fees
- Annual revenue: $100-$300

**Investments:**
- Asset management fees
- Annual revenue: $200-$500

### Product Penetration

**Average Products per Customer:**
- Checking: 100%
- Savings: 60-70%
- Credit card: 40-60%
- Loan: 30-50%
- Investment: 20-40%
