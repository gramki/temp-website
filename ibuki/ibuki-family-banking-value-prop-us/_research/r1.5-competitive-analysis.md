# Research: Competitive Analysis - Mid-Sized US Banks

**Research ID:** R1.5  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** README.md - Executive Overview

## Research Question

What family banking features do mid-sized US banks (PNC, Regions, Fifth Third, KeyBank, Huntington) currently offer?

## Key Findings

Mid-sized US banks offer basic family banking features (joint accounts, authorized users) but lack comprehensive family-aware financial management. No bank offers AI-powered family banking agents or proactive family financial coordination.

## Detailed Findings

### PNC Bank

**Family Features:**
- Joint checking/savings accounts
- Authorized users on credit cards
- Account linking for transfers
- Basic family account aggregation (limited)

**AI/Technology:**
- Basic chatbot for customer service
- Mobile app with voice commands (limited)
- No AI-powered family financial management
- No proactive family insights

**Gaps:**
- No family goal tracking
- No family expense coordination
- No family financial planning tools
- No family context awareness

### Common Patterns Across All Banks

**What Exists:**
1. Joint accounts (universal)
2. Authorized users (universal)
3. Basic account aggregation (most banks)
4. Mobile apps with basic features (universal)
5. Customer service chatbots (most banks)

**What's Missing:**
1. Family context awareness
2. Family goal tracking and alignment
3. Family expense coordination
4. Proactive family financial insights
5. AI-powered family financial agents
6. Family crisis management tools
7. Life transition support
8. Multi-member family financial planning

### Competitive Opportunity

**First-Mover Advantage:**
- No bank offers comprehensive AI-powered family banking
- Market gap is significant
- Technology is available to build this
- Customer demand exists (based on pain points)
