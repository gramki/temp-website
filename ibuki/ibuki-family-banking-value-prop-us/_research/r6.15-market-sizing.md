# Research: US Market Sizing

**Research ID:** R6.15  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What is target segment size, adoption rate assumptions, and growth projections for US family banking?

## Key Findings

For mid-sized bank with 2M households, target segment is 400,000-600,000 households (20-30%). Year 1 adoption: 20,000-60,000 (5-10%). Year 3 adoption: 140,000-300,000 (35-50%).

## Detailed Findings

### Target Segment

**Definition:**
- Families with $50K-$150K income
- Digital-savvy
- Multi-product relationships
- Family coordination needs

**Size:**
- 20-30% of bank's customer base
- 300,000-900,000 households

### Adoption Scenarios

**Conservative:**
- Year 1: 5% adoption = 15,000-45,000
- Year 3: 35% adoption = 105,000-315,000

**Moderate:**
- Year 1: 7.5% adoption = 22,500-67,500
- Year 3: 42.5% adoption = 127,500-382,500

**Optimistic:**
- Year 1: 10% adoption = 30,000-90,000
- Year 3: 50% adoption = 150,000-450,000
