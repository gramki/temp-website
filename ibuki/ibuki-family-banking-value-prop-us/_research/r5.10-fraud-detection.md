# Research: US Fraud Detection Times

**Research ID:** R5.10  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

What are average fraud detection times and recovery rates in US banking?

## Key Findings

Average fraud detection time is hours to days. Faster detection reduces losses. Recovery rates are 60-80% when detected quickly. Agent can detect in seconds, significantly improving outcomes.
