# Research: US Regulatory Compliance Costs

**Research ID:** R6.10  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What are compliance costs for AI agents including testing, monitoring, and regulatory requirements?

## Key Findings

US AI compliance costs: bias testing ($50K-$200K annually), explainability ($100K-$300K annually), monitoring ($100K-$200K annually), audits ($50K-$100K annually). Total: $300K-$800K annually.

## Detailed Findings

### Bias Testing

**Requirements:**
- Regular testing (quarterly/annual)
- Protected class analysis
- Disparate impact testing
- Cost: $50K-$200K annually

### Explainability

**Requirements:**
- Model documentation
- Decision explanations
- Customer-facing explanations
- Cost: $100K-$300K annually

### Monitoring

**Requirements:**
- Ongoing monitoring
- Alert systems
- Remediation processes
- Cost: $100K-$200K annually

### Audits

**Requirements:**
- Regulatory audits
- Internal audits
- Third-party audits
- Cost: $50K-$100K annually
