# Research: US Banking Products Catalog

**Research ID:** R2.11  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** family-banking-product-proposal.md - Market Need

## Research Question

What are the types, fees, and features of US banking products (checking, savings, credit cards, loans, investments, insurance)?

## Key Findings

US banks offer comprehensive product suites: checking ($0-$15/month fees), savings (0.01-5% APY), credit cards (various rewards), loans (mortgages, auto, personal), investments (brokerage, managed), and insurance (life, auto, home).

## Data Points

| Product | Types | Fees/Rates | Features |
|---------|-------|------------|----------|
| Checking | Basic, Premium, Student | $0-$15/month | Debit card, online banking, mobile app |
| Savings | Basic, High-yield, Money market | 0.01-5% APY | Interest earning, limited transactions |
| Credit Cards | Rewards, Cashback, Balance transfer | 15-25% APR | Rewards, cashback, travel benefits |
| Mortgages | Conventional, FHA, VA | 6.5-7.5% (2024) | 15-30 year terms, refinancing |
| Auto Loans | New, Used, Refinance | 5-8% APR | 3-7 year terms |
| Personal Loans | Unsecured, Secured | 8-15% APR | $1,000-$100,000 |
| HELOCs | Home equity lines | 7-9% APR | Variable rate, revolving credit |
| Investments | Brokerage, Managed, Robo | Varies | Stocks, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs |
| Insurance | Life, Auto, Home | Varies | Various coverage levels |

## Detailed Findings

### Deposit Products

**Checking Accounts:**
- Basic: $0-$5/month, minimal features
- Premium: $10-$15/month, enhanced features
- Student: Often fee-free
- Features: Debit cards, online banking, mobile apps, bill pay

**Savings Accounts:**
- Basic: 0.01-0.5% APY
- High-yield: 4-5% APY (online banks)
- Money market: 4-5% APY, check writing
- CDs: 4-5% APY, fixed terms

### Credit Products

**Credit Cards:**
- Rewards cards: 1-5% cashback/points
- Balance transfer: 0% intro APR
- Secured cards: For credit building
- Business cards: For small business

**Loans:**
- Mortgages: $100,000-$500,000+ typical
- Auto loans: $10,000-$50,000 typical
- Personal loans: $1,000-$100,000
- Student loans: Federal and private options

### Investment Products

**Brokerage:**
- Self-directed trading
- Stocks, bonds, ETFs, mutual funds
- Commission-free trading common

**Managed Accounts:**
- Robo-advisors: 0.25-0.5% fees
- Human advisors: 1-2% fees
- Target-date funds

### Insurance Products

**Life Insurance:**
- Term: $20-$50/month for $500K coverage
- Whole: Higher cost, cash value

**Auto/Home:**
- Bundled discounts common
- Varies by location and coverage
