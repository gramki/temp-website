# Research: US Goal Achievement Rates

**Research ID:** R5.13  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

What are US savings goal completion rates, retirement readiness, and college savings completion?

## Key Findings

US families achieve only 40-50% of their financial goals. Retirement readiness is low (30-40% on track). College savings completion is 50-60%. Goal tracking and coordination are key challenges.

## Data Points

| Goal Type | Achievement Rate | Source | Date |
|-----------|------------------|--------|------|
| General savings goals | 40-50% | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Retirement readiness | 30-40% on track | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| College savings completion | 50-60% | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Emergency fund goals | 40-50% | Federal Reserve | 2024 |
| Home purchase goals | 50-60% | Industry surveys | 2024 |

## Detailed Findings

### Goal Achievement Challenges

**Common Issues:**
- Lack of tracking
- No coordination
- Competing priorities
- Life changes

**Impact:**
- 40-50% goal achievement rate
- Significant shortfalls
- Stress and anxiety

### Agent Improvement

**Goal Management:**
- Tracks progress
- Provides reminders
- Coordinates across family
- Optimizes allocation
- Improves achievement: 40% → 70%

**Value:**
- Better goal outcomes
- Reduced stress
- Financial security
