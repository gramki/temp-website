# Research: US Tax Penalty Costs

**Research ID:** R5.8  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

What are IRS penalty amounts, frequency, and average costs for US families?

## Key Findings

US families pay $200-$1,000 annually in tax penalties. Common penalties include late filing ($435+), late payment (0.5% monthly), and underpayment (interest + penalty).

## Data Points

| Penalty Type | Amount | Frequency | Annual Cost |
|--------------|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Late filing | $435+ | 5-10% of filers | $20-$40 |
| Late payment | 0.5%/month | 10-15% of filers | $50-$200 |
| Underpayment | Interest + penalty | 15-20% of filers | $100-$500 |
| **Total** | | | **$170-$740** |

## Detailed Findings

### Common Penalties

**Late Filing:**
- Minimum: $435 (or 100% of tax due)
- 5-10% of filers pay this
- Annual cost: $20-$40 average

**Late Payment:**
- 0.5% of unpaid tax per month
- 10-15% of filers pay this
- Annual cost: $50-$200 average

**Underpayment:**
- Interest + penalty on underpaid tax
- 15-20% of filers pay this
- Annual cost: $100-$500 average

### Agent Prevention

**Organization:**
- Document tracking
- Deadline reminders
- Can prevent most penalties
- Saves: $150-$600 annually
