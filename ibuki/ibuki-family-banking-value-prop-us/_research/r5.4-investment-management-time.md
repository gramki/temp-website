# Research: US Investment Management Time

**Research ID:** R5.4  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

How much time do US families spend on investment decisions and portfolio management?

## Key Findings

US families spend 6-12 hours annually on investment management including research, decision-making, and portfolio review. Many lack clear strategy and spend time inefficiently.

## Data Points

| Metric | Value | Source | Date |
|---------|-------|--------|------|
| Monthly review time | 30-60 minutes | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Annual decision time | 2-4 hours | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Research time | 2-4 hours | Industry surveys | 2024 |
| Total annual time | 6-12 hours | Calculated | 2024 |
| Families with strategy | 30-40% | Industry surveys | 2024 |
