# Research: US Customer Acquisition Costs

**Research ID:** R6.4  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What are customer acquisition costs (CAC) for mid-sized US banks including acquisition channels and costs?

## Key Findings

Mid-sized US banks spend $200-$500 per customer acquisition. Digital channels are lower cost ($150-$300) than branch ($400-$800). Referrals are lowest cost ($50-$150).

## Detailed Findings

### Acquisition Channels

**Digital:**
- Online advertising
- Social media
- Search marketing
- CAC: $150-$300

**Branch:**
- In-person acquisition
- Higher cost
- CAC: $400-$800

**Referral:**
- Customer referrals
- Lowest cost
- CAC: $50-$150

### Agent Impact

**Referral Generation:**
- Satisfied customers refer
- Agent improves satisfaction
- Can increase referrals 2-3x
- Reduces effective CAC
