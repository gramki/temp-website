# Research: US Operations Costs

**Research ID:** R6.11  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What are US labor rates, operations team costs, and support costs for AI agent platform?

## Key Findings

US operations costs: engineering team ($2M-$5M annually), operations team ($1M-$3M annually), support escalation ($500K-$1M annually). Total: $3.5M-$9M annually for 100K customers.

## Detailed Findings

### Engineering Team

**Roles:**
- AI/ML engineers
- Backend engineers
- Integration engineers
- Cost: $2M-$5M annually

### Operations Team

**Roles:**
- Operations managers
- Quality assurance
- Monitoring
- Cost: $1M-$3M annually

### Support Escalation

**Handling:**
- Agent failures
- Complex queries
- Human-in-loop
- Cost: $500K-$1M annually
