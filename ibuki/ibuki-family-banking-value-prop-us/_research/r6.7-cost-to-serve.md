# Research: US Cost to Serve

**Research ID:** R6.7  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What is cost per customer, support costs, and channel costs for US banks?

## Key Findings

US banks spend $200-$400 annually per customer on support and operations. Branch visits cost $150-$300, call center $50-$100, chat $20-$40. Agent can reduce costs by 60-70%.

## Data Points

| Channel | Cost per Interaction | Interactions/Year | Annual Cost |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Branch | $150-$300 | 2-3 | $300-$900 |
| Call Center | $50-$100 | 4-6 | $200-$600 |
| Chat | $20-$40 | 3-5 | $60-$200 |
| **Total** | | | **$560-$1,700** |
| **Average** | | | **$200-$400** |

## Detailed Findings

### Support Costs

**Branch:**
- Highest cost channel
- $150-$300 per visit
- 2-3 visits per customer annually
- Cost: $300-$900

**Call Center:**
- $50-$100 per call
- 4-6 calls per customer annually
- Cost: $200-$600

**Chat:**
- $20-$40 per chat
- 3-5 chats per customer annually
- Cost: $60-$200

### Agent Impact

**Automation:**
- Handles 60-70% of queries
- Reduces human support needs
- Saves: $120-$280 per customer
