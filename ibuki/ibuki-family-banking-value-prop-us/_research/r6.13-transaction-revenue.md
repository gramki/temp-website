**Research ID:** R6.13  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What are interchange fees, transaction fees, and payment revenue for US banks?

## Key Findings

US banks generate transaction revenue: credit card interchange ($200-$500/year per card), transaction fees ($50-$150/year), payment processing ($100-$300/year). Agent can increase transaction volume.

## Detailed Findings

### Credit Card Interchange

**Revenue:**
- Interchange fees: 1.5-2.5% of transactions
- Average card spend: $10,000-$20,000/year
- Annual revenue: $200-$500 per card

**Agent Impact:**
- Can increase card usage
- Better card recommendations
- Higher transaction volume

### Transaction Fees

**Revenue:**
- Wire transfers: $15-$50 per transfer
- ACH: $0-$5 per transfer
- Annual: $50-$150 per customer

### Payment Processing

**Revenue:**
- Bill payment fees
- Transfer fees
- Annual: $100-$300 per customer
