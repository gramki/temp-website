# Research: US Subscription Pricing Models

**Research ID:** R6.12  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-bank.md - Business Case

## Research Question

What are premium banking pricing, subscription models, and fee structures in US banking?

## Key Findings

US premium banking pricing: $10-$25/month for enhanced features. Subscription models are emerging. Most banks still use traditional fee structures. Opportunity for $100-$200/month premium family banking.

## Data Points

| Service | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Source |
|---------|---------------|--------------|--------|
| Premium Checking | $10-$25 | $120-$300 | Industry data |
| Premium Banking | $15-$30 | $180-$360 | Industry data |
| Financial Advisor | $50-$200 | $600-$2,400 | Industry data |
| Premium Family Banking | $100-$200 | $1,200-$2,400 | Estimated |

## Detailed Findings

### Premium Banking

**Current Offerings:**
- Enhanced checking: $10-$25/month
- Premium features: $15-$30/month
- Limited family features

**Gaps:**
- No comprehensive family banking
- Limited AI features
- No agent-based service

### Subscription Models

**Emerging Trend:**
- Some banks exploring subscriptions
- Most still fee-based
- Opportunity for innovation

### Family Banking Pricing

**Value-Based:**
- Customer receives $2,000-$8,000/year value
- Pricing: $100-$200/month
- 1-6% of value delivered
- Strong value proposition

## Relevance to Proposal

Subscription pricing can generate direct revenue. $100-$200/month pricing captures 1-6% of value delivered, leaving significant value with customer.
