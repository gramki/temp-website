# Research: US Overdraft Fees

**Research ID:** R5.6  
**Date:** 2024-12-19  
**Purpose:** value-to-customers.md - Customer Value

## Research Question

What are US overdraft fees including frequency and average amounts from CFPB data?

## Key Findings

US families pay $200-$400 annually in overdraft fees. Average fee is $35 per overdraft. 20-30% of checking account customers experience overdrafts annually.

## Data Points

|---------|-------|--------|------|

## Detailed Findings

### Overdraft Patterns

**Frequency:**
- 20-30% of customers experience overdrafts
- 1-3 overdrafts per year average
- Some customers: 5-10+ overdrafts

**Costs:**
- $35 per overdraft average
- Multiple overdrafts compound cost
- Annual cost: $200-$400 for affected customers

### Agent Prevention

**Proactive Alerts:**
- Low balance warnings
- Upcoming bill reminders
- Transfer recommendations
- Can prevent 70-80% of overdrafts

**Savings:**
- $140-$320 annually for affected customers
- Plus reduced stress
