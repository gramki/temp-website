# Ibuki: Intelligent Family Banking for US Mid-Sized Banks

## A Strategic Proposal for AI-Native Relationship Banking

**Audience:** CEO, EVP, CIO, Board  
**Initiative:** Ibuki Family Banking  
**Market:** Mid-Sized US Banks (1.5-3 million households per bank)

---

## The Opportunity

US families manage increasingly complex financial lives — multiple accounts, diverse products, shared goals, and constant coordination needs. Yet banks continue to serve them as collections of individuals, not as families.

**The gap is massive:**
- 60+ distinct family financial needs remain underserved
- Current banking solutions address only ~40% of family journey needs
- Families spend 45-80 hours annually on financial administration
- No mid-sized bank offers a unified, family-aware financial experience

**The timing is right:**
- AI assistants (ChatGPT, Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant) are normalizing conversational interfaces
- SMS, email, and voice are primary US communication channels
- Families expect proactive, personalized service
- First-mover advantage is available — no mid-sized bank has a family-focused AI banking offering

---

## The Proposal

We propose **Ibuki** — an intelligent family banking platform that deploys AI agents to serve families across any channel they choose.

**Core concept:** Instead of building another app with 60+ features, we deploy a single intelligent agent (branded as **Mira** or **Ally**) that:
- Understands family context, relationships, and goals
- Executes actions through natural conversation
- Anticipates needs and surfaces proactive insights
- Protects family financial security
- Works across ChatGPT, Siri, Alexa, SMS, email, voice, and our own app

**The product is the intelligence, not the interface.**

We request approval to proceed with:

| Phase | Timeline | Investment | Milestone |
|-------|----------|------------|-----------|
| Phase 1 | Months 1-6 | $12.5-$20M | Foundation: Payments domain, Bank App channel |
| Phase 2 | Months 6-12 | $12.5-$20M | Multi-domain: Investments, Loans, Cards |
| Gate Review | Month 12 | - | 20K-60K families, value validation |
| Phase 3+ | Months 12-24 | $10-$15M/year | Multi-channel, full capability |

**Total committed ask:** $25-$40M Year 1, with Year 2 gated on Year 1 results.

**Note on Revenue Model:** Revenue comes from two sources: (1) Direct revenue from subscriptions and increased transaction volume, and (2) Indirect revenue from relationship deepening (deposit growth, cross-sell, churn reduction, cost savings, and LTV improvement). The indirect revenue is the larger component, reflecting the strategic value of becoming the family's primary bank.
