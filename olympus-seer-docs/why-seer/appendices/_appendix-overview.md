# Appendices — Overview

## Purpose

The appendices provide supplementary reference material for readers who need additional depth on specific topics. They are designed to be consulted as needed rather than read sequentially.

## Appendices in This Section

| Appendix | Title | Purpose |
|----------|-------|---------|
| **A** | Glossary | Definitions of all key terms used in this book |
| **B** | Seer + Hub Division of Responsibility | Clear delineation of what Seer owns vs. what Hub owns |
| **C** | AOSM Foundations | Background on the Agent-Oriented System Meta-model |
| **D** | Further Reading | Links to additional documentation and resources |

## How to Use the Appendices

### Appendix A: Glossary

Use when you encounter an unfamiliar term. The glossary provides concise definitions for all acronyms and concepts introduced in this book.

### Appendix B: Seer + Hub Division

Consult when you need clarity on the boundary between Seer and Hub. This appendix provides a responsibility matrix showing which system owns what.

### Appendix C: AOSM Foundations

Read if you want to understand the theoretical foundations of Seer's design. AOSM (Agent-Oriented Systems Modeling) provides the meta-model that underlies Seer's approach to enterprise agents.

### Appendix D: Further Reading

Use to navigate to related documentation for deeper exploration of specific topics covered in this book.
