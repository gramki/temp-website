# Section 13: Summary — Why Seer? — Overview

## Purpose of this Section

This concluding section synthesizes the enterprise agent imperative and Seer's comprehensive response to it. Having examined the requirements in Part 1 and Seer's solutions in the preceding sections of Part 2, this section consolidates the case for why enterprises need a dedicated agent platform and why Seer is designed to meet that need.

This section is intended as both a summary for those who have read the complete book and a standalone overview for executives and decision-makers who need the essential message without the full technical depth.

*   What are the fundamental requirements that distinguish enterprise agents from other AI systems?
*   How does Seer's value proposition map to these requirements?
*   Who should consider using Seer, and under what circumstances?
*   What are the key differentiators between cloud-managed AI services and an enterprise agent platform?
