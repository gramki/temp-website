# Section 21: Persona Twins in Seer — Overview

## Purpose of this Section

This section demonstrates how Seer enables collaborators to create personal AI agents (Persona Twins) that handle tasks, notifications, and scheduled activities on their behalf. Unlike Business Scenarios that handle organizational workflows, Persona Twin Scenarios are personal productivity agents that respond to personal triggers and operate within the delegator's authority boundaries.

Persona Twins extend Seer's agent capabilities to personal productivity, enabling any collaborator (not just developers) to create AI assistants for delegation. Twins follow the standard three-layer agent lifecycle (Raw → Trained → Employed) with special recognition, isolation, and trigger mechanisms that distinguish them from business agents.

## Core Questions Addressed

*   What are Persona Twins, and how do they differ from business agents?
*   How do Persona Twins inherit authority from their delegators?
*   How are Persona Twins created using blueprints?
*   What are the use cases for Persona Twins?

This section is organized into the following sub-sections:
