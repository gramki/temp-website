| Desired Data Point | What Was Searched | Closest Available |

| **Precise vendor-addressable TAM** (what banks pay to tech vendors for payments, net of in-house build) | McKinsey, BCG, IDC, Gartner reports | No single public report isolates this. IDC Banking IT Spending Guide (paywalled) segments by size/region/function and is the best available source. |
| **Spend split by Tier 1/2/3 banks** | Celent, IDC, Gartner, ICBA reports | IDC segments by 4 tiers (paywalled). Public data only available for individual Tier 1 banks (e.g., JPMorgan $20B total tech). Tier distribution above is analyst synthesis. |
