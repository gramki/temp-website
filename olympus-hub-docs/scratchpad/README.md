# Scratchpad

This folder contains **draft ideas and exploratory content** written before they are developed into documented designs with the help of LLMs.

## Purpose

The scratchpad serves as a working space for:
- Initial ideation and brainstorming
- Early exploration of concepts
- Rough notes and incomplete thoughts
- Experimental documentation that may or may not be developed further

## Important Notes

⚠️ **The content in this folder should not be taken seriously as authoritative documentation.**

- Files here are in draft form and may contain incomplete, inconsistent, or speculative ideas
- Content may be outdated, contradictory, or simply exploratory
- This is a "thinking out loud" space for development work

## TODO Files

The `1.TODO`, `2.TODO` files (and future `3.TODO`, `4.TODO`, etc.) contain lists of items to be done. As new todos are discovered, additional numbered TODO files can be added.

## Where to Find Developed Content

If content from this scratchpad is developed into proper documentation, it will be reflected in more organized locations:

- **Olympus Hub documentation**: `olympus-hub-docs/` (organized by subsystem, concept, or guide)
- **Olympus Seer documentation**: `olympus-seer-docs/` (organized by design area and implementation concept)

The scratchpad is intentionally kept separate from the main documentation structure to maintain clarity and avoid confusion between draft ideas and finalized designs.
