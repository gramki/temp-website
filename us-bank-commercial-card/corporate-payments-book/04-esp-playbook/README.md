# Part IV — The ESP's Playbook

Tells the ESP's story — how Apex Payments takes bank-defined building blocks and assembles them into Corporate Payment Products, one per Spend Archetype. Starts with cross-cutting concerns (contracting, variant creation, product assembly, branding, billing) then walks through the product design decisions for each of the four archetypes.
