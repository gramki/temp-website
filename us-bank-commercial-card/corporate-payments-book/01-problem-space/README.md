# Part I — The Problem Space

Frames the corporate payments challenge before any entity is defined. Surveys the landscape of existing solutions, exposes the structural gap between how banks and corporates think about payments, and introduces the two conceptual tools — Spend Archetypes and Spend Mandates — that organize the domain. A bridge chapter maps these concepts to the concrete entities defined in Part II.
