# Part II — The Ontology

Defines every entity in the corporate payments domain — its ownership (bank, ESP, or corporate), its relationships to other entities, its state model, and its role in the three-actor system. Each chapter is a standalone reference for one or more entities, illustrated with Meridian, Apex, and Commonwealth examples.
