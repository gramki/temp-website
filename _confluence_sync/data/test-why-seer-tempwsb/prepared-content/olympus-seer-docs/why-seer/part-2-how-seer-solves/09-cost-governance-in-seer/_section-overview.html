section-9-cost-governance-in-seer-overviewSection 9: Cost Governance in Seer — Overview

purpose-of-this-sectionPurpose of this Section

This section demonstrates how Seer manages and governs agent operational costs. Part 1, Section 5.11 established that cost is a critical operational health and safety signal; this section shows how Seer implements cost governance with production-grade capabilities.

AI agent costs are inherently unbounded and variable. Without governance, a single agent can consume unlimited resources. Seer treats cost not merely as a financial metric but as a safety signal.

core-questions-addressedCore Questions Addressed

  • Why is cost treated as a safety signal?
  • How does Seer provide cost observability?
  • What is the Cost-to-Health Ratio (CHR), and how is it used?
  • How are cost ceilings and budgets enforced?
