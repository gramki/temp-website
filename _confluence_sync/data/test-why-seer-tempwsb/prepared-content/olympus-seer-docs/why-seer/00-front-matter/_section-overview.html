intended-audienceIntended Audience

This document is written for enterprise architects, technical leaders, and decision-makers who are evaluating or implementing AI agent capabilities within their organizations. The primary audiences include:

Audience Primary Interest
Chief Technology Officers (CTOs) Strategic platform decisions, risk posture, long-term viability
Chief Information Officers (CIOs) Enterprise architecture fit, governance, compliance
Enterprise Architects Technical design, integration patterns, capability mapping
Product Managers Capability requirements, feature alignment, roadmap planning
Technical Decision-Makers Build vs. buy analysis, platform selection criteria

The document assumes an intelligent reader with general technology leadership experience but does not assume prior exposure to agent-oriented systems design or enterprise AI agent platforms specifically.

reading-guidanceReading Guidance
