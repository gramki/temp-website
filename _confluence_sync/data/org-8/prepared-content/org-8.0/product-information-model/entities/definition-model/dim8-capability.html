Capability

Model: Definition Model
Dimension: Dimension 8: The Structural Dimension (Topology)
Owner: Enterprise Architects, Business Analysts

Definition

A high-level ability provided to the user or system — a functional grouping of related Features within a Module.

Purpose

Provides the mid-level taxonomy between Module and Feature. Capabilities represent "what the product can do" at a level meaningful to both business and technical stakeholders.
