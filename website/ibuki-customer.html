What Mira promises

The way a good relationship is supposed to feel.

“I’ll remember, so you don’t have to.”

Renewals, bills, goals, the things that slip — Mira holds them so they never become a problem.

“I’ll never leave you stuck.”

A declined card, a shortfall, a missed payment — every problem arrives with a way through it, not a dead end.

“I work for you first.”

Mira will tell you to cancel something your bank sells if it’s wrong for you. Your interest comes first — always.

“You’re always in control.”

Mira suggests and, with your okay, acts. Every action is yours to approve, and yours to undo.

Your money, your rules

Powerful help, with you in charge of all of it.

Mira can do a lot on your behalf — which is exactly why you decide what she can do, and can change your mind anytime.

You grant, you revoke

Decide what Mira and any family member can see or do. Change it, or take it back, in a tap.

Nothing happens without you

Mira asks before she acts on anything that matters, and everything she does can be undone.

Privacy by default

Your family shares what you choose to share — and nothing you don’t. A child never sees a parent’s salary.

A real person, always

Whenever you want a human, the path is one step away. Mira never traps you in a loop.
