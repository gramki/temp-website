01Where the value sits

Optimizing individuals is linear — and capped. Optimizing the movement of work changes the institution.

A dispute that takes eleven days contains perhaps ninety minutes of human work. The rest is movement: queues between teams, handoffs that shed context, reconciliations between systems that disagree, escalations, and status meetings about all of the above. The proportions repeat across onboarding, credit decisions, exception handling, and product launches.

When a commitment is resolved end-to-end by a governed combination of people and agents operating on one model of the work — context never shed, state never re-keyed — the edge doesn't get faster. It ceases to exist as work. The handoff was never the job; it was the overhead of dividing the job among parties who couldn't hold it whole.

02The vocabulary that describes a bank

You cannot build what you cannot describe.

Ten words, each naming work and its movement — not tools and their users. Each unlocks a question that today has no owner.

The work itself
Work Model
The explicit map of a domain's work — its commitments, disciplines, and the scenarios through which work gets resolved.
"What is all the work of this domain?"
Stream
A commitment to the outside world, fulfilled end-to-end — across teams, systems, and divisions.
"What do we owe, end to end?"
Loop
An internal discipline run continuously — reconciliation, fraud vigilance, compliance assurance.
"What keeps this domain healthy?"
Resolution and orchestration
Hub
A bounded business domain — product, distribution, relationship, operations — running on its Work Model.
"Who owns this domain's transformation, end to end?"
Swarm
A governed team of AI agents, trained on the Work Model, employed with identity, delegated authority, and guardrails.
"Who — exactly — is doing this work?"
The dial
The explicit, reversible division of every piece of work between people and agents.
"How much is absorbed — and what moves next?"
The estate and accountability
Machine
Any system — yours, ours, anyone's — enrolled into the Work Model behind a stable Tool Contract.
"What can this domain call on?"
Induction
The enrollment of your existing systems — wrapped, registered, intelligence extracted; nothing ripped out.
"What do we keep?"
Fabric
A single-concern, contract-exposed system of record or capability — the building blocks the estate evolves toward.
"Can we change one thing without breaking ten?"
Decision record
The replayable account of every consequential decision: what was decided, on what evidence, under whose authority.
"Can we defend this to the regulator?"

Every one of these words survives a change in technology. Models will improve, vendors will come and go, protocols will be replaced — and the bank's Streams, its dial positions, its decision records remain meaningful. That is the test of enterprise vocabulary: it describes what persists.

03Four rungs

Each level of org becomes agent-native: designed around the work, not around scarcity of attention.

RUNG 1

A service domain, agent-native

A cardholder's own assistant files a dispute at 6:40 a.m. — agent to agent. The bank's servicing Swarm verifies the delegated authority, gathers evidence, evaluates it against policy, and issues a provisional credit before breakfast. The floor behind it is smaller and more senior: the Swarm resolves the volume; people resolve what deserves people. The eleven days were the edges. The edges are gone.

RUNG 2

A product domain, agent-native

A product becomes a blueprint in a Hub. Variants configured, simulated, launched under governance in days. The annual calendar becomes weekly. Products for segments of one — delivered through agent channels and narrow direct influence — become operable.

RUNG 3

A banking division, agent-native

A division becomes a federation of Hubs. The headcount plan becomes a set of dial positions. Management becomes directing work and owning guardrails. The division head runs the business from a scorecard — denominator, absorption, outcomes, cost per resolution.

RUNG 4

A bank, agent-native

Governed domains on a fabric estate. The middle office of pure coordination dissolved. People concentrated where a bank differentiates. An institution legible from the outside — to its regulator, its partners, and its customers' agents, who are met deliberately under verified delegation.

The difference: ten thousand copilots is today's bank, accelerated. An agentic bank is a different bank.
