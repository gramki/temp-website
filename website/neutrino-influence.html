03Three new relationship models

From individual to household to agent-native participation.

Individual Relationships

The core relationship
  • Person with verified identity, account access, product holdings
  • Deposits, credit, cards held individually
  • Influence reaches them through their preferred channels: app, email, agent, ad feed
  • RM engages them through the RM console

Household Relationships (Eon)

The family financial operating system
  • Multi-member household with delegated authority (parent, spouse, adult child)
  • Shared goals, household budgets, individual pocket money, family banking
  • Parents see children's accounts; children see parental controls; spouses coordinate
  • One household profile across all surfaces — influence reaches the right person for the decision

Agent-Native Relationships (BYOA)

The customer's agent, your concierge, RM all coordinating
  • Customer's agent (Apple, ChatGPT, corporate treasury AI) integrated as a delegated agent
  • Your concierge (Ibuki) proactively managing on the customer's behalf
  • RM coordinating across both, seeing agent activity and concierge engagement
  • Fine-grained delegation: agent X can see accounts, concierge can initiate transfers under $10k

06Delegation & governance

Customer controls who influences them and what each agent can do.

Influence is powerful. It must be governed. Fine-grained delegation ensures every agent (yours, theirs, household) operates only within the customer's explicit authorization.

How delegation works

Agent authentication

Every agent (person, Ibuki concierge, customer's Apple agent, fintech partner agent) has a verified identity. The system knows exactly who is acting on the customer's behalf.

Granular authority

Customer grants: "My agent can see checking account balance," "Concierge can initiate transfers under $10k," "RM can view credit utilization," "Partner agent can see product eligibility." Not all-or-nothing delegation.

Audit trail

Every delegation decision (grant, modify, revoke) is timestamped and attributed. Customer can see who has what authority and when it was granted.

Instant revocation

Customer removes agent access in real-time. No delegation lingers; no agent has persistent authority. Revocation takes effect immediately.
