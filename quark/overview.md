## Quark to Neutrino Integration and Domain Mapping
* A Quark Bundle Product can be associated with one or more Digital Experience Subscriptions; 
* A Quark Bundle Product will have an application assement request queue.
* A Quark Bundle Product will have a Issuance Queue
* A Quark Bundle Product is published to all the Neutrino Product Directories under the Digital Expereince Subscriptions the Bundle Product is associated with. 
* When publishing to Neutrino Product Directory, the Application Assessment queue and Bundle Issuance queues are specified for use by Neutrino.
* Quark requires Neutrino;
* Neutrino is agnostic of Quark;


## Neutrino: Product Directory and Product Portal

Multiple Appplication Journeys can be defined for each product in the Neutrino Product Directory. A completed application is submitted to the Product Provider for assessment and issuance. A Product Provider is expected to notify Neutrino about updates to the application assessment requests and issuance requests.

- Application Definitions
- Onboarding Journeys
- Activation

## Neutrino: Offer Portal

Presents a mechanism to discover all offers available to the users under each digital experience Subscription of the bank. The users will be able to discover the offers across all the servicing channels and can avail the offers.
