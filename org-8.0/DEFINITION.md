## What should change deliver?
* Bench/Reserve should be visible
* New Product Line investments should be clear
* Foundry and Olympus should serve as cost centers; Conclude the matter; We are not going to do GTM
* GTM teams' sponsorship of Modules, Capabilities should be clearly called out; This should be an investment decision justified by future revenues or other strategic investment decisions
* SaaS Products should be optimized for customer activation and reusability in various solutions
* Tachyon Suite of SaaS Products must pursue only maturity, reliability, maintainability, and efficiency objectives
* All functional features (domain or customer initiated) should be part of programs aligned with Win initiatives and shouldn't be confused for product roadmap
* There is no concept of intakes to a SaaS team; Customer-facing initiatives are clubbed to a customer-release teams or they are part of the Studio deliverables.
* Data Lake should be an indepdendently billable item
* Maturity teams are the maintaners and reviewrs of all the changes to the SaaS
* Sales/GTM Alignment to reality of the Product Suite - Who we are ready for and What we are ready for (and who & what we are not ready for) 
* Cost of Activation of Customer should be reduced
* Cost of serving use-cases/value-stream (Cost per Account, Cost Per Card, Cost Per Account Holder) should be reduced
* Achieve Effort and Cost Transparency in initiatives pursued for any customer (either upon direct request of the customer or because of our internal design and approach)
