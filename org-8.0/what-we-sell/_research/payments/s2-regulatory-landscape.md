## Key Findings

- All 4 markets have active regulatory mandates with specific deadlines forcing payments infrastructure investment
- India most mandate-intensive market (UPI, tokenization, digital banking directions, PA master directions)
- EU instant payments regulation is strongest forcing function in Europe
- ISO 20022 MX-only era in effect — nearly half of banks were behind on readiness
- Brazil PIX evolving rapidly (Automático, Parcelado, Contactless) — each requires new infrastructure
- Regulatory calendar 2025-2028 is densest ever across all 4 markets simultaneously
