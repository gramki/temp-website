## Gaps Identified

- **UK/Europe is a complete gap.** Need: NatWest, Lloyds, or Barclays ISO 20022 migration status; Commerzbank/ING instant payments projects
- **India card platform modernization missing.** UPI is the only signal; no card issuance or acquiring modernization signals found
- **Brazil limited to Tier 1.** No mid-market or neobank signals (Nubank, Inter, C6 Bank technology investment)
