## Key Findings

- India (UPI) and Brazil (PIX) represent most advanced real-time ecosystems by adoption depth
- USA has greatest infrastructure complexity (two competing networks), strongest orchestration demand
- Europe's regulatory mandate (EU IPR Oct 2025) is most aggressive forcing function
- ~60% of banks have or are implementing payment hubs
- Payment orchestration market growing 24% CAGR
- Named bank modernization examples: Metropolitan Commercial Bank (Finzly), Vantage Bank (Finzly), Berkshire Bank (Finastra)
