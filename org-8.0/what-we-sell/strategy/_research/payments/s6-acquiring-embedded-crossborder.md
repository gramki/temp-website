## Key Findings

- Acquiring diversification: ISV-integrated payments eating traditional merchant acquiring
- Cross-border cost and speed remain unsolved — the most fragmented segment
- Embedded payments growing 35%+ CAGR — banks face disintermediation risk
- Private payment networks represent an emerging category, poorly tracked by research firms
- India a remittance superpower ($129B received) with active cross-border infrastructure investment
- ISO 20022 is a de facto infrastructure mandate across all geographies

## Gaps and Unresolved Questions

- All 20 claims lack navigable URLs — need Stream 7/8 verification
- Private payment networks sizing is estimated, not directly sourced
- Embedded payments revenue "at risk" to banks is modeled, not empirical
- Cross-border cost per transaction varies widely by corridor — one average may mislead
