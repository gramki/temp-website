## Key Findings

- Vendor-addressable payments tech market: $60-85B narrow / $150-200B broad (2025)
- Tech layer growing 3-4x faster than payments revenue (14-18% vs 4% CAGR)
- Payment orchestration fastest-growing segment at 24% CAGR
- Tier 2-3 banks heavily vendor-dependent, primary growth market
- USA: 30-40% of global spend; India: 6-10%; Europe: 18-25%; Brazil: 3-6%

## Gaps and Unresolved Questions

- No single public report cleanly isolates vendor-addressable TAM
- IDC Banking IT Spending Guide (paywalled) is the definitive source for tier-level segmentation
- India and Brazil tech vendor spend poorly separated from transaction values in public reports
- All 35 claims lack navigable URLs — need Stream 7 verification
