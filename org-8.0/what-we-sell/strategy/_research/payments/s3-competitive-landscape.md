## Key Findings

- FIS-TSYS / Global Payments-Worldpay swap is the defining competitive event of 2025-2026
- Mid-size and regional banks are the most underserved segment — legacy too expensive, modern too fintech-oriented
- 8 competitive gaps identified, centered on mid-market bank payment modernization
- Cloud-native payments hubs displacing legacy middleware (Volante, ACI Connetic, Form3)
- India/Brazil structurally different: government-backed rails shift vendor value to orchestration/VAS
- 27 vendors profiled across 5 categories
