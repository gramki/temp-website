## Key Findings

- Tokenization is transitioning from a security layer to a platform infrastructure layer
- Bank-issued token strategies lag behind network-level tokenization (Visa/Mastercard)
- Modern card issuance platforms (Marqeta, i2c, Lithic) threaten legacy issuer processors
- Virtual cards growing fastest in B2B, commercial cards
- India mandated tokenization creates infrastructure urgency by April 2026
- Private payment networks (retailer-branded) represent $200B+ in closed-loop payments value
