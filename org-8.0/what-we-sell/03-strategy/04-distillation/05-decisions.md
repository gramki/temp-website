# Chapter 04.05: Decision Log

*A living register. Started June 2026.*

---

## 1. Purpose

The distillation chapters deliberately end in questions — [portfolio deliberations](01-engagement-area-summary.md#7-the-deliberation-agenda) and [CEO-motion deliberations](03-ceo-gtm.md#8-deliberation-questions) — because those judgments belong to leadership, not to analysis. This chapter is where the answers live.

The rule: **a question, once decided, is closed here and not re-litigated in new chapters.** New analysis that challenges a closed decision does so by reopening the entry (with the trigger noted), not by silently contradicting it. This keeps the book coherent as it grows and gives every future author one place to check what is already settled.

## 2. Record Format

Each decision is one row in the registers below. Substantial decisions may additionally get a short record appended in Section 5 using this template:

```
### D-NNN — <title>
- Date / Status: <YYYY-MM-DD> / Open | Decided | Reopened | Superseded
- Question: <the question as originally posed, with source link>
- Decision: <what was decided>
- Rationale: <why — the judgment, not a re-run of the analysis>
- Owner: <accountable function or person>
- Revisit trigger: <the event or date that reopens this>
```
