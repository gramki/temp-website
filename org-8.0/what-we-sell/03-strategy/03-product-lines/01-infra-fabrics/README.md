# Chapter 03.01: Infra Fabrics

Domain-neutral enterprise fabrics for cloud-native, agentic systems.

---

## Overview

Infra Fabrics provide foundational capabilities that banking applications need but that are not specific to any banking domain. They address cross-cutting concerns: identity and trust, data integrity, cloud operations, system evolution, AI governance, and intelligent automation.

These fabrics enable Zeta's Banking Fabrics and Banking Product Lines to operate as governed, observable, evolvable systems. They are the infrastructure layer that makes agentic banking possible.
