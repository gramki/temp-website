# Chapter 03.01.04: Neutrino — Channels and Channel Products

**Channel infrastructure and channel products — providing the interaction surfaces through which customers, agents, and AI participate in banking operations across all domains.**

---

## Product Family

Neutrino is Zeta's channels and channel products family. It provides the infrastructure for building, composing, and operating the interaction surfaces through which banking work is accessed — by customers, by bank staff, and by AI agents.

In Hub Way terms, Neutrino delivers **Channels** — the collaboration surfaces through which humans and agents participate in a Hub's Scenarios. Neutrino also delivers **Channel Products** — organization-scoped experiences that recompose components from multiple Hubs into cohesive experiences for end users (e.g., a customer's mobile banking app drawing from Payments, Servicing, and Credit Hubs).

## Relationship to Fabrics

| Fabric | How Neutrino Uses It |
|---|---|
| **Evolution Fabric** | Neutrino channel components register as Channels in domain Hubs — each Hub configures which Neutrino channels are available for its Scenarios. Channel Products compose components from multiple Hubs into unified experiences |
| **Trust Fabric** | Customer and agent authentication, session management, consent capture, and channel-specific identity verification |
| **Truth Fabric** | Consistent entity presentation across channels — ensuring that the same customer, the same balance, the same status means the same thing regardless of which channel surfaces it |
| **Cognitive Audit Fabric** | Auditability for channel-mediated interactions — what was presented, what was consented to, what actions were taken through which channel |
| **Cloud Fabric** | Infrastructure management, observability, and operational reliability for Neutrino deployments |
