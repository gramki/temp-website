# Business KPI

**Model:** Definition Model
**Dimension:** Dimension 2: Business Value Dimension (Vendor Economics)
**Owner:** Product Marketing, Executives

## Definition

The internal health metric for the vendor — a quantitative measure used to evaluate the commercial success of the product.

## Purpose

Provides the measurable targets that product and business teams use to assess whether the product is achieving its vendor-side economic goals.
