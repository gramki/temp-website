# Value Metric (Pricing Axis)

**Model:** Definition Model
**Dimension:** Dimension 2: Business Value Dimension (Vendor Economics)
**Owner:** Product Marketing, Executives

## Definition

The strict unit of measurement that dictates cost to the customer. The pricing axis along which revenue scales.

## Purpose

Defines the commercial mechanism that ties product usage to revenue generation. Critical for aligning product decisions with business outcomes.
