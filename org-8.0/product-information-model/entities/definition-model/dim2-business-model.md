# Business Model

**Model:** Definition Model
**Dimension:** Dimension 2: Business Value Dimension (Vendor Economics)
**Owner:** Product Marketing, Executives

## Definition

The fundamental revenue engine that describes how the organization building the software generates income.

## Purpose

Anchors the Vendor Economics dimension by establishing the macro-level commercial structure. All other entities in this dimension (Pricing Tiers, Value Metrics, KPIs) derive from the Business Model.
