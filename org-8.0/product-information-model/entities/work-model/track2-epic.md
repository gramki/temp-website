# Epic

**Model:** Work Model
**Track:** Track 2: The Build Track (Construction)
**Owner:** Tech Lead, Developers

## Definition

A large, committed capability — a significant body of work decomposed from a PSD that delivers a coherent chunk of product functionality.

## Purpose

The top-level work entity in the Build Track. Epics are the direct decomposition of PSDs (Dimension 1) into buildable units. They contain User Stories and Technical Tasks.
