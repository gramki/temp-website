# Objective Setting Task

**Model:** Work Model
**Track:** Track 1: The Discovery Track (Learning)
**Owner:** Executive Leadership, Product Management

## Definition

Work to define or refine strategic Objectives for a planning horizon. Involves reviewing business strategy, board directives, market conditions, and performance data to establish clear, measurable strategic goals.

## Purpose

Makes the strategic planning work explicit in the Work Model. Objectives (Dimension 1) don't appear out of thin air — they require deliberate effort to define, refine, and align across leadership. This entity tracks that work.
